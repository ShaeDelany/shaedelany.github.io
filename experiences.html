Best Experiences of 2025

Traveling to Emory University

The CMS volleyball team traveled to Atlanta, Georgia to compete in the NCAA regionals for the national championship. CMS first played Transylvania University, where the girls won in five sets. It was a close match but the team pulled through. The volleyball team fell short to emory in the semi-finals which placed the Athenas tied for 16th in the nation.

Beating Pomona-Pitzer

The Pomona-Pitzer sage hens and the CMS Athenas have a long history where they are rivals. It was a great moment for the girls to crush their rivals in a 3 set sweep. The sagehens had no chance against the Athenas this year.

Traveling to Saint Louis

During preseaon, the Athenas traveled to Washington University in Saint Louis to compete against the some of the best teams in the nation. They played against Tufts Univeristy where they defeated the elephants in five sets. The CMS team also defeated the Trinity Tigers and lost to the Emory Eagles.
